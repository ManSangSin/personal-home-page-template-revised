About Me

Hello world, thank you for visiting my page. From a young age, I have always enjoyed playing about with technology. I have a vivid memory of my older brother unboxing his computer (Packard Bell) and how amazing it was with its huuuge 20GB IDE hard drive running Windows Millenium. Many many hours was wasted playing solitaire and 3D pinball whilst listening to Winamp and watching its cool visualisations. Move along around a decade, I watched as my brother built his first custom PC. A few more years after that was when I was lucky enough to build my own PC.

Now, with my many years of experience geeking about with computers; has pretty much made me the tech guy of the household. Any technical issues, or purchasing of anything technology related; they would normaly consult with me first which, I honestly do not mind helping as I enjoy anything technology related tbh.

Currently I am working on an online course which is taught by google over on Coursera about Google IT Automation with Python. I have learned a lot of different concepts including "Python", Git" and IT automation using "Puppet". I have thoroughly enjoyed the course so far and would like to continue doing something similar.
